Georgia Fowler is a prominent model from New Zealand. Georgia Fowler has also appeared in the music video of the song “It Ain’t Me” by Kygo and Selena Gomez. As a model, she has walked the runway for brands like Chanel and Victoria’s Secret. She has also appeared in numerous magazines.

Early Life and Childhood

Georgia Fowler was born on June 18, 1992, and she is currently 31 years old. She is originally from Auckland, New Zealand and her zodiac sign is Gemini. Talking about her family, her parents are her father Peter Fowler, and her mother Kim Fowler. Her father is an Australian golfer. She also has a sister named Kate Fowler. Both she and Kate attended Diocesan School for Girls. It is a private girls’ school in Epsom, Auckland, New Zealand.

Career and Professional Life

Professionally, Georgia is a model who is popularly linked with Victoria’s Secret. She has also walked the runway for Chanel and has been featured in publications like Harper’s Bazaar Australia and Vogue Girl Korea among others. She has also worked with brands like the Banana Republic and H&M.

Initially, both she and her sister Kate were discovered when she was only 12 years old. They later signed with a modeling agency in Auckland. After four years, she signed with IMG and hence moved to New York for her career. IMG is the same company that signed Naomi Campbell, Gisele Bundchen, and other high-profile models.

Moreover, she gained immense fame after she walked the runway for the Victoria’s Secret annual fashion show for three consecutive years 2016, 2017, and 2018. She was also the presenter of Season 1 of Project Runway New Zealand in the year 2018.

More Details

Georgia Fowler has been quoted saying that Victoria’s Secret was her goal that she had been working towards for five years. Hence, she shoots with this brand regularly. Further, Fowler has also worked for high-profile fashion lines like Miu Miu, DKNY, and Prada among many others. She has also walked the runway for Chanel and Kanye West’s Yeezy label.

Fowler has also been on the cover of Harper’s Bazaar Australia three times! She then went on to star in a music video for the song It Ain’t Me. It is a song by Kygo and Selena Gomez. Moreover, Fowler was prominently featured at the Mercedes-Benz Fashion Week in Australia in 2018. She also hosted the Australian Fashion Laureate Award alongside Ash Williams.

Since the year 2018, Fowler has been listed on models.com as a “Money Girl”. In the years 2017 and 2018, she appeared in magazines like Marie Claire Australia, Vogue, The File, W, and Harper’s Bazaar. She appeared in these many magazines to share her beauty and fitness regimen.
