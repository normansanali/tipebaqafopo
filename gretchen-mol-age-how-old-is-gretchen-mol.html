Former American model Gretchen Mol is currently 50 years old.

Mol was born in November 1972.

Who Is Gretchen Mol?

Gretchen Mol is an actress, and former model known for playing Gillian Darmody in the TV series Boardwalk Empire from 2010 to 2014.

Born on November 8, 1972, in Deep River, Connecticut, Mol attended The American Musical and Dramatic Academy in New York.

Upon graduation, she began a career in modelling, worked with several well-known fashion brands, and appeared on the cover of magazines such as Vanity Fair and Esquire.

Mol later embraced acting and got her breakthrough role in the 1998 film “The Last Time I Committed Suicide.”

Since then, Mol has starred in many other films, including “Rounders,” “Celebrity,” “Cradle Will Rock,” “The Notorious Bettie Page”, The Funeral, Donnie Brasco, Rounders, Sweet and Lowdown, The Thirteenth Floor, 3:10 to Yuma, and Manchester by the Sea.

She also appeared on TV shows such as “Boardwalk Empire,” “Life on Mars,” and “Mozart in the Jungle.”

Mol has won a number of accolades including a Golden Globe award nomination for her role in “The Notorious Bettie Page”.
