Doug Baldwin net worth and salary: Doug Baldwin is an American football wide receiver for the Seattle Seahawks who has a net worth of $1.8 million. Doug Baldwin played college football for the Stanford Cardinal from 2007 to 2010 where he led the Stanford team in receiving yards and touchdowns his senior year. Doug Baldwin caught his first NFL touchdown in the first week of the 2011 NFL Season. As a rookie, Baldwin was 4th in reception yardage in the entire NFL and led his team in both receiving yards and receptions. Doug Baldwin was the first undrafted rookie free agent to lead his team in receptions and receiving yards since the AFL-NFL merger in 1970.
