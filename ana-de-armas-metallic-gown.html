Ana de Armas Looks Like a Goddess in a Shimmering Metallic Gown

 Posted on August 9, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  316 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Ana de Armas made a dazzling fashion statement during the red-carpet premiere of The Gray Man last night.

The actress stepped out wearing a shimmering metallic gown featuring a sequined bustier and a silvery reflective skirt made of strips of geometric shapes. She kept the rest of her ensemble in line with the metallic theme, opting for silver strappy heels and diamond drop earrings.

Steve Granitz//Getty Images

Michael Kovac//Getty Images

Also present at last night's premiere were de Armas's Gray Man costars Ryan Gosling and Chris Evans.

Gosling wore a bright blue suit jacket with mint green pants, styled with a white dress shirt and white shoes. Meanwhile, Evans looked suave in a black suit, white T-shirt, and dark shades.

David Livingston//Getty Images

De Armas recently opened up about living under public scrutiny as a celebrity in Hollywood for ELLE's August 2022 cover story.

Referring to how publicized her relationship with Ben Affleck was in 2020, the actress said the experience was "horrible" and "one of the reasons why I left L.A."

The constant barrage of paparazzi and the media storm that followed made her realize she needed to leave California. "Going through it [myself] confirmed my thoughts about, 'This is not the place for me to be,'" she said. "It became a little bit too much. There's no escape. There's no way out. It's always the feeling of something that you don't have, something missing. It's a city that keeps you anxious."

De Armas currently lives in New York with her boyfriend, Tinder executive Paul Boukadakis.
