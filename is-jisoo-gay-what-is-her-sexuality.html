Is Jisoo gay? This question has been a topic of interest among music lovers. The Blackpink member’s sexuality is straight, and she is not dating anyone.

Jisoo, a talented South Korean singer and actress, rose to fame as a member of the internationally renowned girl group Blackpink, which YG Entertainment formed in August 2016.

Alongside fellow members Jennie, Lisa, and Rose, she contributes her remarkable vocal abilities and charismatic presence to the group’s success.

Her journey in the entertainment industry began when she was introduced as the third member of Blackpink on June 15, 2016. Before her debut, she joined YG Entertainment as a trainee in July 2011.

Notably, she made memorable appearances in Epik High’s “Spoiler + Happy Ending” (2014) from their album “Shoebox” and Hi Suhyun’s music video for “I’m Different” (2014).

Beyond her musical works, she ventured into acting, debuting with a cameo role in the 2015 series “The Producers.” Her breakthrough in the acting world came with her foremost leading role in the JTBC series “Snowdrop” (2021–22).

She was honored with the Best Actress Award at the 2022 Seoul International Drama Awards.

Is Jisoo Gay – What Is Her Sexuality? 

Jisoo’s sexuality has always sparked curiosity among fans, leading to speculations about whether she is gay. This sensation is common in the Korean entertainment industry, where rumors about celebrities’ sexual orientations frequently arise.

The Blackpink member has found herself at the center of such rumors, but it should be noted that she identifies as straight. She has not made any public statements indicating otherwise.

Moreover, it is highly unlikely that any Blackpink members are gay. Jisoo, in particular, shows no signs of being bisexual, as there is no evidence of her engaging in intimate relationships with women outside of the other three members of Blackpink.

It’s important to recognize that her bond with them resembles that of sisters.

Jisoo Dating Timeline 

Blackpink member disclosed that she has never experienced dating or being involved in a serious relationship. Due to the restrictions set by her agency, she has not been involved in any romantic relationships.

In a revealing interview in 2016, she shed light on the strict rules enforced by YG Entertainment and its founder, Yang Hyun-Suk, regarding dating.

During an appearance on JYP’s Party People in 2017, the members of Blackpink confirmed the existence of a “dating ban” within their company, YG Entertainment.

However, whether this ban is still in effect as of 2023 remains uncertain. She shared a memorable meeting with Yang Hyun-Suk during their initial meeting.

He directly inquired about her relationship status and even noticed a ring she wore, prompting her to clarify that it was merely an accessory, not a symbol of a romantic attachment.

As for her love life, she has maintained that she has never been romantically involved or engaged in a committed relationship.

Jisoo Bio explored

Kim Ji-soo aka Jisoo was born on January 3, 1995, in Gunpo, Gyeonggi, South Korea. She was grown alongside her older brother and sister.

During her childhood, Ji-soo actively engaged in sports and martial arts, particularly basketball and Taekwondo. Her respect for the Korean boy group TVXQ was evident, showcasing her interest in music at a young age.

She debuted solo with the single album “Me” on March 31, 2023. The album proved a massive success, skyrocketing to the top of the Circle Album Chart and selling a staggering 1.03 million copies in under two days.

It made her the highest-selling female soloist in South Korea and the first to surpass the one million mark in album sales.

She secured a spot among the top K-pop idols in various surveys. In 2018 and 2019, she ranked 10th and 17th in Gallup Korea’s annual surveys, solidifying her status as a beloved artist.

Before her debut with Blackpink, Ji-soo appeared in advertisements for renowned brands such as Samsonite, Smart Uniform, LG Electronics, and Nikon.
