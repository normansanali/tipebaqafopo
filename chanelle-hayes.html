Chanelle Hayes is a famous British television personality, glamour model, singer, and businesswoman. Chanelle Hayes is famous for her work in The Friday Night Project, Celebrity Juice, and Big Brother Uncut.

Early Life

Chanelle Hayes was born on 11 November 1987. Her birth name is Chanelle Jade Hayes. She is from Wythenshawe, Manchester, England. Moreover, she was born at HM Styal Prison. She holds British nationality and follows Christianity. She is 35 years old currently and her zodiac sign is Scorpio.

Chanelle was just 4 months old when her birth mother, Andrea Sinclair was murdered on 5 April 1988 by a client, Keith Pollard. Her mother was a 32-year-old drug addict and street prostitute. After the incident, Chanelle was adopted by Christine and Harry Hayes and was raised in Wakefield, West Yorkshire.

When she was 15, she sought information about her birth family from social workers. Then, in Sheffield, she reunited with her grandmother and her sisters. Further, she was a student at NEW College, Pontefract, West Yorkshire, studying Spanish, music, and English.

However, in August the organizers of the V festival declined her request to sing. Likewise, in June 2008, she began a column in the glossy weekly magazine Star.
