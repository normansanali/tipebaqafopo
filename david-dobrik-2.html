David Dobrik aka David Julian Dobrik is a Slovak YouTube star, comedian, podcaster, actor, and social media personality. As of 2022, David Dobrik’s net worth is $25 million. He accumulated his net worth through advertisements and brand collaboration on his social media accounts. He is popularly known for being the co-founder of a popular image-sharing application for iPhones named Dispo.

He mainly rose to fame after his videos went viral on the Vine platform and after that, his videos got popular on the YouTube platform in the year 2015. He is also the leader of the popular ensemble on YouTube named The Vlog Squad.

Career

David Dobrik started his career as a content creator on the video-sharing platform Vine in the year 2013. He started making interesting fun videos on this platform and after that, he moved to the YouTube platform where he also uploads interesting fun videos along with other content creators like Liza Koshy, Gabbie Hanna, Jason Nash, and others.

He is also a member of the popular YouTube group named Second Class. In the year 2015, he launched his self-titled YouTube channel and in the very next year, he has launched his second YouTube channel named David Dobrik Too on which he posts comedy vlogs, challenges and prank videos, and sponsorships deals.

He also has a podcast titled View along with fellow YouTubers like Jason Nash, Joe Vulpis, and Natalie Mariduena. In the year 2017, he launched his third YouTube channel named Views Podcast. In the year 2019, he has been featured in a TV reality show titled America’s Most Musical Family as a judge along with Ciara and Debbie Gibson.

In the year 2019, he also appeared as a co-host in the TV reality show titled 2019 Teen Choice Awards along with Lucy Hale, and later in the same year, he appeared as one of the presenters at the American Music Awards of 2019 and also made a recurring guest appearance in the TV series Chopped Junior.

In the year 2020, he co-launched a mobile application for IOS named Dispo. In the same year, he has also made his appearance in the TV series titled The Tonight Show and become the host of the popular TV reality show titled Dodgebell Thunderdome on the Discovery channel. Later in the year 2020, he launched a perfume brand named David’s Perfume, the same year he has co-hosted several reality shows and award shows.

David Dobrik has been featured in many popular music videos like Sad Song, My Life Sucks, Cozy, Graduation, 25 Christmas Trees, and other popular music videos.
