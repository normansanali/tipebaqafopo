Will Collins: Revolutionizing Speed Performance Training with Fast University

 Posted on July 30, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  603 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Will Collins, the owner of Fast University, has emerged as a trailblazer in the realm of speed performance training. His profound commitment to empowering top athletes and revolutionizing the track and field economy has propelled Fast University to the forefront of the industry. Through his unwavering dedication and strategic partnerships, Collins has not only fostered a support system for elite athletes but also established Fast University as a beacon of excellence in the world of speed training.

The Vision of Fast University

Fast University stands as a premier resource for athletes dedicated to enhancing their speed and performance. The core mission of the organization revolves around providing a robust support system for top athletes engaged in speed training. By working with some of the world's most esteemed athletes and collaborating with professionals across various sports, Fast University has cemented its status as a leader in the field.

  • The organization collaborates with top athletes globally and consistently produces individuals who are considered the fastest in their respective age ranges.

  • Fast University's expertise extends to working with professional bobsledders, NFL athletes, including the renowned Pittsburgh Steelers star running back Najee Harris.

  • In addition to athlete-focused initiatives, Fast University has forged partnerships with the Swiss Olympic Team and NFL teams, offering consultation on cutting-edge sprinting and hurdling techniques.

The Journey of Will Collins

Will Collins' journey to becoming a prominent figure in speed training is marked by resilience, determination, and a deep-rooted passion for the sport.

  • Born into a military family in South Korea, Collins later relocated to the Fort Hood Army Base in Killeen, TX, where he was raised amidst challenging circumstances.

  • Overcoming initial struggles, Collins nearly abandoned track and field before securing a personal track trainer in his senior year, a turning point that propelled him to secure a scholarship at the esteemed University of Houston, renowned for its track and field program.

  • Dissatisfied with the coaching approach at the university, Collins departed after a year, embarking on a 12-year journey to immerse himself in speed and performance training, laying the foundation for the inception of Fast University.

Fast University's Evolution and Impact

Collins' unwavering commitment to revolutionizing speed training culminated in the establishment of Fast University, a testament to his perseverance and dedication to the sport. Embracing a multifaceted approach, Collins delved into the realms of marketing and branding, leveraging his expertise to propel Fast University's online presence and cultivate a burgeoning social media following.

  • The overarching goal of Fast University is to catalyze a paradigm shift in the track and field economy, advocating for greater recognition and rewards for athletes.

  • Collaborating with legendary sprinter Justin Gatlin, Collins seeks to effect meaningful change in the track and field landscape, envisioning groundbreaking events that can reshape the industry.

  • Central to Collins' vision is the empowerment of track athletes, enabling them to build personal brands and garner year-round visibility, transcending the confines of traditional Olympic cycles.

The Legacy of Will Collins

Will Collins stands as a testament to unwavering determination and relentless pursuit of excellence. His remarkable journey from a challenging upbringing to becoming a venerated entrepreneur in the realm of sports exemplifies resilience and vision. Through Fast University, Collins has not only redefined speed performance training but has also championed the cause of athletes, striving to elevate their standing within the track and field community.

In essence, Will Collins epitomizes the epitome of dedication and innovation, leaving an indelible mark on the world of speed training and athletics. As an entrepreneur, Collins has emerged as one of the hardest-working visionaries, reshaping the landscape of sports through his unwavering commitment to excellence and empowerment.
