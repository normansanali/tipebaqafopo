Ansel Elgort and Violetta Komyshan broke up after a decade of an on-and-off relationship. Despite the ups and downs they faced, Komyshan stood by Elgort as he gained fame following his breakout role in The Fault in Our Stars. She supported Elgort during significant Hollywood events, including award shows and movie premieres.

The former couple was known for their public displays of affection on red carpets and in social media posts during the early stages of their romance. However, they chose to keep their relationship low-key, with only a few public outings, especially after rumors of their split in 2020, which followed allegations of assault against Elgort.

Why Did Ansel Elgort Break Up With Girlfriend Violetta Komyshan?

During the Thierry Mugler: Couturissime event at the Brooklyn Museum in November 2022, Violetta Komyshan confirmed to E! News that she and Ansel Elgort have separated. Komyshan did not reveal the reason behind their split, but she expressed her single status and mentioned that she finds it enjoyable.

When asked for advice for other single women, the professional ballerina stated, "Focus on your work and your passions. I'm 26, so right now is the age to push ahead and focus on that—on yourself."

In 2015, Elgort expressed his preference for long-term relationships and the importance of trust in a girlfriend during an interview with ELLE. He also implied his relationship with Komyshan, citing the qualities he values in a girlfriend by referencing their shared interests and compatibility.

Ansel Elgort And Violetta Komyshan's Careers Got In The Way Of Their Relationship
