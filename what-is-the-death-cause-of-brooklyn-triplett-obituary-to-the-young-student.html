What Is The Death Cause Of Brooklyn Triplett? Obituary To The Young Student

 Posted on August 28, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  407 words  |  Aldo Pusey

What is the death cause of Brooklyn Triplett? Here’s what we know about the deceased Tishomingo High School student.

Brooklyn Triplett was a Tishomingo, Oklahoma resident.

She reportedly died in an accident last Tuesday. Many people poured their sadness and gave condolences to the bereaved family.

Brooklyn was a softball player who was part of many teams, including the Knockouts, Extreme, Crushers, and Chaos. Moreover, she was a cheerleader for Davis.

Brooklyn Triplett Obituary And Death Cause

Tishomingo High School student Brooklyn Triplett died on Tuesday, 22 March 2022.

As for the cause, it was an accident. A four-seater car, Chevy Spark, collided with a semi-truck in Tishomingo. Besides her, three others, Gracie Machado, Austin Holt, Memory Wilson, Madison Robertson, and Addison Gratz, passed away.

The car’s driver was a 16-year-old, while three of the girls were 15 and the other two were 17.

The Tishomingo Public School’s superintendent Bobby Waitman informed about the incident via a Facebook post.

The Johnston County Sheriff’s Office received a call to check out a wreck at US 377, State Highway 22, and State Highway 99. A semi-truck heading south on US 377 collided with a car heading east on OK-22.

The truck driver is 51-year-old Valendon Burton from Burneyville, Oklahoma. He didn’t get any injury as his seat belts were on. Moreover, he didn’t receive any charges.

Ohio Highway Patrol is continuing the investigation.

Following the tragic news, thousands of people expressed their grief and sorrow online. We hope Brooklyn’s soul rests peacefully and her family gets enough strength to bode the situation.

As for the funeral, none of the sources have disclosed the details, including the date and venue. However, a community candlelight vigil for the young victims is on 7 p.m. Friday at the Tishomingo High School football field.

A team of Kelly Jackson, Tamara Garrison, and Kristen Hall organized a fundraising campaign at GoFundMe yesterday. So far, 36 people have donated a total of $1,755.

Brooklyn Triplett Parents

Brooklyn Triplett’s parents are Mark Triplett and Adrienne Triplett.

Her father, on Facebook, changed his profile picture, paying tribute to his deceased daughter. Likewise, her mother also did the same.

Her mom is a Sulphur High School and Murray State OK graduate. Her parents celebrate their anniversary on 12 November each year. Aside from that, there isn’t much about the two online.

In addition to her parents, Brooklyn also has a kid brother. Likewise, her grandparents are Ronnie Triplett and Renee Webb Triplett.
