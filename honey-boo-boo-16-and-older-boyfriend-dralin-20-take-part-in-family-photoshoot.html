Honey Boo Boo, 16, and older boyfriend Dralin, 20, take part in family photoshoot

 Posted on July 30, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  755 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Alana ‘Honey Boo Boo’ Thompson looked like a proud aunt during a family photoshoot in Los Angeles, that also included her boyfriend Dralin Carswell. 

The star, 16, beamed next to her beau, 20, as she posed with sister Lauryn ‘Pumpkin’ Efird, 22, her husband Josh, 25, their newborn twins – son Sylus Ray and daughter Stella Renae, six weeks – older son Bentley, 11 months, and daughter Ella, four. 

The adorable photos, taken on Wednesday, come following news that the sisters might get to star in their own reality TV show without their mother, June Shannon, 42, after Lauryn was awarded permanent custody of Alana. 

Happy family: Alana ‘Honey Boo Boo’ Thompson, 16,  looked like a proud aunt during a family photoshoot that also included her boyfriend Dralin Carswell, 20

Alana looked casual for the day, sporting a colorful tie-dye T-shirt and wearing her blonde tresses down.

Meanwhile her older beau wore an olive T-shirt and two gold chains, and appeared to fit right in with the family. 

Supermom Lauryn matched her sister with a laid-back look that consisted of a graphic T-shirt and khaki shorts.

Parents of four: Her sister Lauryn ‘Pumpkin’ Efird, 22, and husband Josh, 25, showed off their newborn twins, six weeks, older son Bentley, 11 months, and daughter Ella, four

Her husband wore a similar outfit, and further accessorized with a chunky silver chain and a black hat.

The loved-up couple – who were married in 2018 – were also seen giving each other a sweet kiss on the lips while holding their twins, who wore pink and blue onesies. 

Meanwhile their older daughter looked precious in a pink romper, while her red-haired brother Bentley sported a blue T-shirt and khaki shorts like his parents.

Sealed with a kiss! The couple – who were married in 2018 – gave each other a sweet kiss on the lips while holding son Sylus Ray and daughter Stella Renae 

Lauryn certainly has a fair share of parental responsibilities on her hands as a mom of four, and Alana’s primary care taker. Her sister Jessica Shannon, 25, also lives with her.

She recently talked about her hectic life with The Sun, saying: ‘Things have been stressful adjusting to three babies under one year old and just five kids in general but it’s nothing me and Josh can’t handle. This just means life is a little more hectic but will be so worth it in the end.’

The publication also reported that Lauryn and Honey Boo Boo might be getting a new reality series of their own soon. 

It comes after last month Mama June reportedly lost custody of daughter Honey Boo Boo after a court battle in Georgia with Lauryn.

Drama: Mama June Shannon’s (left) daughters Lauryn (right) and Alana (center) are reportedly set to star in their own reality TV show without their mother, 42; Pictured in 2018

An insider told the publication of the possible new show: ‘They are in talks about having their own show and they will be meeting about it in the next few weeks.’ 

The source added that the family is currently on their way to LA, although it’s unclear if that is where the meeting will take place. 

It comes after last month Lauryn was awarded permanent custody of the Here Comes Honey Boo Boo star, according to The Sun which claimed to look at court papers filed in Georgia. The final ruling was handed down in April.

Family: According to The Sun, the sisters are meeting with producers later this month to discuss a new series that would follow the drama of their daily lives; Pictured in NY in 2017

Testimony from the hearing revealed the former Toddlers And Tiaras cast member had been living with Lauryn and her husband, Josh Efird, 25, since 2019.

The Mama June: From Not to Hot star will be allowed visitations, with permission from Lauryn, and must provide $800 a month in child support payments until Alana turns 18.

June – who recently married boyfriend Justin Stroud, 34, after a few months of dating – lost custody of the teen in 2019 after she and former boyfriend, Geno Doak, 49, were arrested on drug charges.

Pumpkin received temporary custody in August of that year, and the teen has been living with her ever since. Court documents show both sides agreed to the arrangement.
