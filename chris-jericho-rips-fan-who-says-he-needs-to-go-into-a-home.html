Chris Jericho Rips Fan Who Says He Needs To Go Into A Home

 Posted on August 12, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  253 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Chris Jericho is a true veteran in the professional wrestling world, having been a part of it for thirty years now. Jericho has shown time and time again that he is very selfless when it comes to putting over talent. That said, it appears he hit back at a fan who said he needs to go into a home.

Le Champion has faced off against numerous talented pro wrestlers over the course of his four years in AEW. Jericho has never failed to put over talent such as MJF, Orange Cassidy, Action Andretti and more. He even lost to all of the above talent and more in order to establish them.

The former AEW World Champion has also developed quite a reputation in the past couple of years, as many fans feel he has become on edge and resorts to childish attacks in response to criticism.

A fan took to Twitter and stated that Chris Jericho needs to go into a home. Chris Jericho saw the fan’s tweet and clearly didn’t too kindly to what the fan said, as he replied in an expected way.

Agreed! I’ll go into YOUR home and spend some quality time with yer mum…

Chris Jericho and Sting also had a major confrontation on this week’s episode of AEW Dynamite. We will have to wait and see how their feud will progress in the coming weeks.
