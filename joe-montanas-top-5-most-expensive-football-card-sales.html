Joe Montana's Top 5 Most Expensive Football Card Sales

 Posted on August 31, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  522 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

By Jocelyn Alano July 02, 2024 23:28

credit. steelcitycollectibles

A football legend in his own right, Joe Montana made history. Not only is he a 4-time Super Bowl Champion, but his reach in the game of football has secured his memorabilia as highly sought after by collectors. For this list, we break down Joe Montana's top five football card high-sales.

 

The Iconic 1981 Topps #216 Joe Montana PSA 10

The most iconic and valuable Joe Montana card is the 1981 Topps #216 Joe Montana PSA 10. This card, which captures the young gunslinging QB on the brink of championship contention, reached an all-time high of $114,000 in an April 2022 PWCC auction. Its neon green outline and sleek design give it an allure that has stood the test of time.

 

Dual Montana/Brady Autograph /25 PSA 10

Featuring two bold signatures of football legends in a PSA 10 holder, this card symbolizes the passing of the torch from one generation to the next. It last sold for over $30,000 in 2022.

 

The $31,799 2004 Topps Insert Autograph

This neat insert autograph from Topps contrasts two generational talents and features two signatures into the mix. It's a hand-numbered 1/1 and last sold for nearly $32,000 in a May 2022 eBay auction.

 

The 1985 Topps Football Joe Montana #157 PSA 10 GEM MINT

This 1985 Topps football card graded PSA 10 GEM MINT sold for an impressive $1,530 on July 23, 2023. Its pristine condition and historical significance contribute to its high value.

 

The 1982 Topps Football Joe Montana #488 PSA 10 GEM MINT

Graded PSA 10 GEM MINT, this 1982 Topps football card fetched $660 in a PWCC Auction on August 7, 2023. Its rarity and excellent condition make it a highly coveted piece for collectors.

How to Discover the Most Valuable Joe Montana Football Cards

To find out the most valuable Joe Montana football cards, collectors can keep an eye on reputable auction houses like PWCC Auctions and Goldin Auctions. These platforms often showcase rare and valuable sports cards, providing insights into the ever-changing landscape of card values. Additionally, staying updated on the latest sales and trends in the sports memorabilia market can help collectors identify the most sought-after Joe Montana cards.

In conclusion, Joe Montana's impact on the sport has not only secured his place in football history but also made his football cards highly valuable and sought after. The allure of owning a piece of history, combined with the scarcity and pristine condition of these cards, continues to drive their prices into the five-figure range and beyond. For collectors and enthusiasts, the quest to own one of these coveted Joe Montana football cards is not just a pursuit of memorabilia but a tangible connection to one of the greatest quarterbacks in NFL history.

As a sportswriter, it's always exciting to witness the intersection of sports and collectibles, and Joe Montana's football cards exemplify the timeless appeal of sports memorabilia. Whether you're a seasoned collector or a casual fan, the allure of owning a piece of history in the form of these iconic cards is an experience like no other.
