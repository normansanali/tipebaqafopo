Where Is Tiki Outpost Island In Blox Fruits?

 Posted on August 12, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  460 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Looking for the Tiki Outpost island in Blox Fruits update 20? We don't blame you — here's how to find it!

First Published October 22, 2023, 11:03

Blox Fruits Update 20 has finally arrived! So what does that mean? Well, it means tons of new content is being added to the game — ranging from the Mammoth Fruit to new weapons like the Shark Anchor. But that's not all!

There's also a new biome called Tiki Outpost Island. This mysterious land has much to offer if you know where to find it. So, get ready to set sail, chums! Here is everything you need to know to locate the Tiki Outpost in Blox Fruits update 20.

How To Find Tiki Outpost Island In Blox Fruits Update 20

In order to reach the Tiki Outpost Island, you will first need to go to Hydra Arena in the Third Sea. When you are there, set your bearings directly North. Next, travel straight, and you will eventually get to the docks at the Tiki Outpost Island in Blox Fruits.

As you approach the land, you'll see the docks as well as a large white and grey arch made of rock. You'll also spot a large temple in the middle of the island. Note that this is a Level 2450 zone  — if you're not strong enough, you might struggle to battle the enemies.

What Is The Tiki Outpost Island In Blox Fruits?

The Tiki Outpost Island is a new zone filled with new NPCs and enemies. Let's break it down for you!

Inhabitants and NPCs

  • Spy
  • Beast Hunter
  • Shark Hunter
  • Shafi
  • Dragon Talon Sage

Enemies

  • Island Outlaw
  • Sun-Kissed Warriors
  • Island champion
  • Island boy

In addition to these inhabitants, you can also unlock the Sanguine Art Fighting Style in Blox Fruits Update 20. This is a blood-based fighting style that you get from Shafi. And that's all you need to know about the Tiki Outpost Island in Blox Fruits. We hope you found this guide useful!
