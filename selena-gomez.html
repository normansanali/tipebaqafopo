This is how I think of the song. Its not that long to read although it looks it. There is lyric by lyric explanation, unless the line is completely obvious. This is how the song would relate to me..

"You make me so upset sometimes
I feel like I could loose my mind"
- pretty self explanatory. But it says that she cares about him so much that even the littlest things he says can make her feel sad or upset.

"The conversation goes nowhere
Cause you never take me there"
- We never talk about anything serious because nothing will ever come out of us being anything.

"And I know what i know
And I know that you're no good for me
Yeah, I know what i know
And I know it's not meant to be"
- She knows that he isn't the right guy for her.

"He's my dilemma
One half me wants ya
And the other half wants to forget
- They used to have something special but something went wrong so now even though she wants to move on and forget about everything she can't because he is special to her.

"And I tell myself to run from you
But I find myself attracted to my dilemma"
- She knows that she should turn away from the whole situation but something keeps her staying there.

"Your eyes have told a thousand lies
But I believe them when they look in mine"
- Even though she knows that he lies a lot, she still wants to believe him. She wants to believe that he won't lie to her anymore.

"I heard the rumors but you won't come clean
I guess I'm hoping it's because of me"
- She knows that the rumors are true, even though he denies them. She wants to think that he won't admit to it because he doesn't want her to think of him in a bad way.

" could live with you
Your smile, your eyes
The way you make me feel inside
I could live with you
But I don't wanna "
- If she had to live without him she could. But she rather deal with the pain that hes puts her through.
