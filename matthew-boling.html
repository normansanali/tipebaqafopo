Matthew Boling- Biography

Matthew Boling was born in the United States on June 20, 2000. American runner who is known for competing in the 400, 200, and 100-meter dash sprint events as well as the 4×100 relay event. With record-breaking scores and unprecedented comebacks, he quickly became the number 1 sprinter and track and field athlete in the nation in 2019 with a 100-meter time of 9.98 seconds. He is known for having challenged professional NFL player Ted Ginn Jr. to a 10,000-dollar race in May of 2019 through Twitter. He started gaining attention when he started racing in the 400-meter dash. He attended Houston Strake Jesuit high school where he competed on their Track and Field team. He then signed a National Letter of Interest with the University of Georgia.
