Rodney Mathews Job

Rodney Mathews is an experienced sales representative with a demonstrated history of working in the medical device industry.

As of May 2018, Rodney has been employed with Cintas as their outside sales representative. Cintas, in their words, has been helping businesses of all types and sizes get Ready for the Workday with products and services that help them keep their facilities clean, safe, and looking their best.

Rodney graduated with an associate’s degree from Citrus College where he listed football captain as an activity. Later, he transferred to California State University, Fresno, where he continued his football career. There, from 2013 – 2015, he also studied for his bachelor’s degree in Mass Communication/Media Studies.

So, during his collegiate career, he was a defensive back for the bulldogs. He played his university’s first two games before suffering a season-ending knee injury. And just like that, his dreams of playing in the NFL were shattered.

Nevertheless, Rodney picked himself up and redirected his ambition, working as an intern at ABC 30 Action News, associate sales representative at Smith & Nephew, and a service advisor at Toyota North America, one after the other. Also, over the years, he learned to enjoy his profession.
