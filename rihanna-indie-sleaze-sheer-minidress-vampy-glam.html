Rihanna Embraces the Indie-Sleaze Trend in a Sheer White Minidress

 Posted on August 14, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  339 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Rihanna took her street style worldwide for Paris Fashion Week.

On Friday, the singer attended the A$AP Rocky × American Sabotage by AWGE Spring 2025 menswear show to support the rapper, who is her partner of roughly four years and father to their sons RZA and Riot Rose. For the occasion, RiRi opted for an edgy white minidress that looked like the high-fashion version of a Hanes tank top, complete with garter elements at the hem.

Stephane Cardinale - Corbis

Despite the warm weather, she layered with a burgundy puffer jacket, taking care to show off its white feather lining. Beneath the cropped jacket, an army-green garment and a piece of red and gray fabric peeked out, adding dimension to the Grammy winner’s look. Rihanna wrapped up her ensemble (quite literally) with a pair of white leather stilettos, which had silver-studded leather straps wrapping around her feet and ankles.

Stephane Cardinale - Corbis

The Fenty Beauty mogul brought the distressed look of her outfit full circle with her accessories. Seeming to channel the 2010s, RiRi wore a gold choker chain and three delicate cross necklaces, as well as a number of diamond cuff earrings and studs. She carried a bedazzled white purse and threw on a pair of bold red aviator sunglasses as she left the show.

Her glam mimicked the era her jewelry came from with a bold hair look—a stark contrast to how she styled her natural hair for the Fenty Hair launch party earlier this month. Single-handedly bringing back an indie-sleaze classic, Rihanna wore her dark hair long and straight, highlighted with platinum blonde streaks in a deep side part.

She complemented the look with vampy makeup, including a smoky eye shadow and glossy burgundy lip, which matched her cropped jacket. Inside the event, Rihanna sat front row, for the best look at the designs created by her beau.
