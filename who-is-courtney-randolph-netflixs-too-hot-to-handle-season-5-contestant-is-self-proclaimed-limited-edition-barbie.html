HOUSTON, TEXAS: A group of determined singles are all set to once again land in the dating world of 'Too Hot to Handle.' These sensuous men and women will try their best to mingle in paradise. However, at the same time, they will have to remain celibate in order to win $100,000. Any breach of the rules will result in the reduction of the final prize money.

In Netflix's 'Too Hot to Handle' Season 5, we will see irresistible men and women co-existing together under one roof. Indeed, the temptation of making out with their potential match will be high. Throughout the show, we will see digital assistant Lana bringing in new tasks for the contestants to keep the storyline interesting. As we wait for the release of the new season, let us take a look at who is the new sizzling contestant Courtney K Randolph.

Who is Courtney Randolph?

Courtney K Randolph is a 25-year-old real estate agent, model, reality star, and  content creator. She is a Houston, Texas, native. Courtney says about herself, "I’m a limited edition Barbie, I come with two looks a bikini and a blazer." Being a model, the H-town beauty is often seen flaunting her chiseled body in bikinis. Courtney also owns a LOC growth oil business named Courtney Randolph Collection. It claims to nourish, restore, and strengthen the locks. Courtney wears her flaws like a badge. She says, "Show me something natural like (a peach emoji) with some stretch marks."

Courtney has confidence in herself. She says in the trailer, "As a woman you are very powerful, can get anything you want." She then adds, "You just have to learn how to tap into it." In every season, 'Too Hot to Handle' goes to extreme lengths to cover its tracks. The frustration of a morning boner or thwarted sexual desire will make the contestants go crazy over each other. Courtney is seen kissing one of the contestants in the trailer. This indeed means that she will not be getting the full prize money if she wins the show.
